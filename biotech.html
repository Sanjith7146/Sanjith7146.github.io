In-Vitro Diagnostics Workshop

Workshop from Stratificare on IVD as part of the SST Discover Programme for the Secondary 4s. During the lab sessions, we experimented using a specific IVD test called the ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay). ELISA is a plate-based assay technique designed for detecting and quantifying soluble substances such as peptides, proteins, antibodies, and hormones. We conducted ELISA to test a protein expressed by the Dengue Virus.

A video to showcase parents and students one of the applied subjects offered only in SST, Biotechnology.
