Introducing Tamiliva

Struggling with Oli Verupadu? No phrases for Tamil Composition? Hard to pronounce some tamil letters? No form of quizzes to test your understanding? Fear not! Tamiliva is here.

Tamiliva allows you to learn, favourite and search up Oli Verupadu, learn Tamil letter pronunciation and composition phrases, take notes and test your understanding in the quiz! Therefore, you can learn from your mistakes in the quiz and master all these skills by yourself. With our app, you can now unlock you full potential for the Tamil Language!

Features in Tamiliva:
  • Oli Verupadu (2 or 3 Words with the same sound):
    • Search up words in Tamil and English
    • Favourite some words
  • Letters pronunciation
  • Sotrodargal (Phrases):
    • Thirukkural
    • Phazhamozhi
  • Notes
  • Quiz

My Role in this project

Tamiliva was created by Sri, Tanish and myself under the ProjectSF programme in SST Inc. You can visit the website here sstinc.org

I was the Co-Founder, the Chief Design Officer (CDO) and the Assistant Chief Technological Officer (ACTO) of Atom Inc.

As the CDO, it was my job to design the app. I had some trouble figuring out which modifiers I could use to add the needed design elements. I also found it a struggle to design a good and simple app logo that represents our app effectively as it was my first time designing a logo. I consulted seniors and friends about how I can better design the app logo. I have learnt Logo Desgining and the skills of collaboration, commitment, leadership and research through this project.

As the ACTO, it was my job to code out and collect data for the Oli Verupadu section. It was tedious to collect and cross-check the data as there were roughly 240 words and to look through all of them was not an easy task. I also learnt many things while coding out the Oli Verupadu and researched a lot on my own to find out how to create things like searchbars and custom navigation bar displays.

Overall, it was a truly enriching experience to code out Tamiliva and this experience has taught me a lot of new skills.
